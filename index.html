About us

The Islamic Charity Center Association was established in 1963 and registered in the Ministry of Social Development in 1965 by a group of loyal Jordanians who dedicated themselves to charitable work.

The Islamic Charity Center Association is focused on five main areas:

  • Food distribution
  • Schooling programs
  • Emergency and Disaster Response
  • Development Cooperation
  • Orphan Sponsorship
It is all about creating generations that have belief, confidence and knowledge.
